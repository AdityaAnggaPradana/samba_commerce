Project Summary

The project's primary purpose is to create a powerful analytical tool in the form of a company-wide dashboard. This tool will enable Samba Commerce to analyze its performance comprehensively, optimize operations, and make data-driven decisions to support ongoing growth and success in the competitive e-commerce market

Project Background


Samba Commerce, founded in 2021, is a dynamic and fast-growing e-commerce platform that has quickly become a prominent player in the Brazilian market. Samba Commerce offers more than 15,000 products across over 50 product categories to customers all over Brazil. Several internal and external variables have contributed to our business growth. In 2022, Samba aims to leverage more data to further support our expansion. The CEO, has tasked to the Head of Data, and his team with creating a company-wide dashboard. The purpose of this dashboard is to provide various charts for analyzing the company's performance, enabling a better understanding of our business operations. The overarching goal is to capture and comprehend a broader range of metrics, thereby enhancing the comprehensiveness and depth of our data insights

Objectives

Create a comprehensive company-wide dashboard for Samba Commerce that provides insights and metrics to support the growth and achievement of the company's main OKR related to transactions in 2022. With Key Elements of the Objective:

  1. Collect and integrate data from various sources within the organization.
  2. Design and develop an interactive and user-friendly company-wide dashboard that visualizes key metrics related to the company's main OKR for 2022, with a primary focus on transactions.
  3. Identify and present key metrics that are essential for understanding and improving the company's performance related to transactions.

Data Integration: Connecting Datasets using Left Join in Looker Studio


  1. Combine Order History, Orders, and Payments tables using a left join on 'Order ID .
  2. Left join allows us to include all records from the main table (Orders) and matching records from the related tables (Order History and Payments). This comprehensive view ensures no data is lost during integration

Monthly Total Orders Vs Monthly Total Payment Value


Interpretation:
  1. Overall, there appears to be a strong correlation between monthly orders and total payment value. As the number of orders increases, the total payment value also tends to rise. This indicates that customers are making larger purchases during periods of higher order volume.
